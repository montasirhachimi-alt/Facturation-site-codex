## CRM Business Model

The current B2B CRM model is company-centric.

Company is the commercial account. Contacts belong to Companies. Quotes, Invoices, Payments and Opportunities belong to Companies.

Customer remains as an internal compatibility layer for legacy persistence fields and future B2C or advanced account structures, but it is not exposed as an independent workflow in the current product experience.

See `docs/architecture/ARCH-001-COMPANY-CENTRIC-CRM.md`.
