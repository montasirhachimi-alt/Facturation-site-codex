# SPR-330 — CRM Home Visual Cleanup: Quick Actions

## Executive Summary

SPR-330 cleans up the CRM Home quick actions section after product owner feedback. The sprint removes the isolated floating plus treatment and makes quick actions feel like one calm, compact and professional BOSIACO section.

No Runtime, Services, business logic, Prisma, database, APIs, permissions, backend behavior, AI or workflows were changed.

## What Was Visually Wrong

- The floating plus buttons looked isolated from the card content.
- The plus treatment added decorative noise without improving navigation.
- The card alignment felt less premium because the top-right affordance competed with the action title.
- The quick actions read as five separate decorative cards instead of one intentional section.

## What Changed

- Wrapped CRM Home quick actions in a single `Actions rapides` section card.
- Added a clear section header and short explanatory copy.
- Removed all floating plus bubbles from CRM Home quick action cards.
- Reworked each quick action as a compact aligned card with:
  - left icon,
  - title,
  - short helper text,
  - subtle right arrow affordance.
- Preserved every existing route and click behavior.

## Desktop Result

On desktop, the quick actions now align as a compact five-column action row inside one grouped panel. The section reads as a coherent CRM control area instead of separate decorative cards.

## Mobile Result

On mobile and tablet, the cards stack or flow into two columns with consistent row structure, no floating controls, and clearer tap targets.
