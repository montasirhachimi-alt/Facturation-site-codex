# SPR-312 — Company Contacts Workspace

## Summary

SPR-312 connects the Contact domain to the Company Workspace. The Contacts tab inside `/crm/companies/[companyId]` is now operational.

## Objective

Create the first connected CRM workflow: Company → Contacts.

Contacts remain inside Company Workspace. No standalone Contacts page was added.

## Connected CRM

The Company Workspace is the CRM center. Contacts are displayed, searched, filtered, added, edited and archived only in the context of a selected Company.

## Company → Contacts Relationship

The workspace passes the active `companyId` to `ContactService`.

The UI uses:

- `ContactService`
- Contact domain types and validation
- CRM Shared Foundation for search, sort and pagination
- Enterprise UI Framework for table, toolbar, dialog, stats and feedback states

## Workspace Integration

The existing `Contacts` tab now renders `CompanyContactsWorkspace`.

The route remains:

- `/crm/companies/[companyId]`

## Reuse Strategy

No table, dialog, toolbar, pagination or empty state was duplicated outside the Enterprise UI Framework.

Business logic remains in `ContactService`.

- Future actions for activities, emails and meetings remain placeholders.
