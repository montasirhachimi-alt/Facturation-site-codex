# SPR-329 — Product Identity Foundation

## Executive Summary

SPR-329 creates a reusable BOSIACO visual language so Dashboard, CRM and Sales feel like one premium SaaS platform instead of separate page families. The sprint is product-experience only. It does not change Runtime, Services, business logic, database, Prisma, APIs, permissions, backend behavior, AI or workflows.

## Design Language Decisions

- Standardize the page rhythm around: Hero, Business Insights, Primary Actions, Main Content and Secondary Information.
- Use a dark executive hero as the recognizable BOSIACO focal point on high-value workspaces.
- Keep navy for confidence, blue for action and focus, white cards for clarity and semantic colors only for status meaning.
- Use large rounded surfaces, deliberate whitespace and stronger shadows as the BOSIACO card language.
- Use status chips with small dot indicators so states feel consistent across CRM and Sales.
- Use section headers with icon tiles to make page scanning calmer and more recognizable.
- Keep table density business-friendly while giving tables a branded title band and stronger row focus.

## Visible Changes

- Dashboard now uses the same BOSIACO hero and insight language as CRM and Sales.
- Quick actions use the same large tactile card pattern across modules.
- Business KPIs share the same premium metric card component.
- Sales pages now open with launch-grade heroes instead of generic entity headers.
- Sales tables now have branded dark title bands and stronger hover focus.
- Sales status badges now match CRM badge rhythm with dot indicators.
- Filter and search controls use consistent larger radii and calmer spacing.
- The app shell has a subtle product background that makes screenshots feel more cohesive.
