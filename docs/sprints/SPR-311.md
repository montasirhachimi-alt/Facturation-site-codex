# SPR-311 — CRM Contacts Foundation

## Summary

SPR-311 creates the complete CRM Contact domain foundation.

Contacts are company-scoped records. They are not standalone CRM objects.

## Objective

Add pure TypeScript Contact domain types, constants, validation, utilities and in-memory service without adding UI, pages, APIs, Prisma or database persistence.

## Domain Model

Each `Contact` belongs to exactly one Company through `companyId`.

The model includes:

- workspace scope
- company relationship
- first name, last name and full name
- job title and department
- email, mobile phone and office phone
- preferred language and timezone
- status and role
- primary contact and decision-maker flags
- LinkedIn profile
- notes and tags
- owner and audit metadata

## Relationship With Companies

Contacts consume `CompanyId`, `WorkspaceId` and `UserId` from the Company domain.

This keeps Company Workspace as the center of CRM and prepares future attachment of Contacts to account workspaces.

## Workspace Awareness

`ContactService` scopes every list, search and lookup operation by `workspaceId`.

## Permission Awareness

Contact operations accept optional structured permission decisions and support the existing `crm.contact.read` / `crm.contact.write` permissions.

No new permission engine was introduced.

## Future Integrations

The Contact domain is prepared for future relationships with:

- Activities
- Emails
- Meetings
- Tasks
- Sales Opportunities
- Projects
- Invoices
