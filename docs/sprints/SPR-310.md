# SPR-310 — Company Workspace 2.0

## Summary

SPR-310 upgrades the Company Workspace from a details page into an enterprise-grade CRM workspace.

## Objective

Make each company feel like the operational home of the CRM account without adding Contacts, Sales, Projects, Prisma, APIs or backend behavior.

## Workspace Philosophy

Company is the central CRM entity. The workspace should give users a daily place to understand context, recent interactions, upcoming work and future business relationships.

## UX Decisions

- The header now behaves like a premium account hero.
- Favorite and pinned actions are visible placeholders.
- Executive KPI cards show future module attachment points.
- Overview moved below operational context so the page feels action-oriented.
- Relationship graph shows how future CRM, Sales, Projects and Billing modules will connect.
- Timeline, notes and tasks use mock data only.
- Inspector panel gives a compact right-side account context.
- Tabs were refined to feel more deliberate and enterprise-ready.

## Future CRM Integrations

Future modules can attach to this workspace:

- Contacts
- Customers
- Opportunities
- Quotes
- Orders
- Projects
- Invoices
- Activity
- Notes
- Files

## Known Risks

- Timeline, notes, tasks, revenue and relationship graph are mock placeholders.
- No backend persistence exists for workspace interactions yet.
- Future modules must connect through existing CRM architecture instead of embedding business logic in the workspace UI.
