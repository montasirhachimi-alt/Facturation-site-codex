# SPR-334 — Professional Workspace Optimization

Date: 2026-07-09

## Objective

Transform BOSIACO from a premium presentation UI into a more efficient professional workspace. The sprint reduces unnecessary vertical space, improves above-the-fold density and keeps the established BOSIACO visual identity intact.

## Scope

- Product experience only.
- No Runtime, Services, API, Prisma, database, permission or business logic changes.
- No new modules, workflows or backend features.
- Existing routes and click behavior are preserved.

## Workspace Optimizations

- Product heroes now keep their executive tone with shorter vertical rhythm, reduced internal padding, calmer shadows and more compact action rows.
- The top application bar uses tighter controls and spacing so the working canvas begins sooner.
- KPI cards retain the BOSIACO accent and icon treatment while reducing excess padding and improving density.
- Entity tables expose more rows by reducing header, cell and empty-state padding.
- Search, filter and toolbar primitives are more compact while preserving clear click targets.
- CRM and Sales workspace cards and contextual panels use tighter spacing for daily-use productivity surfaces.

## Desktop Improvements

- 1440px, 1600px and 1920px layouts benefit from less decorative vertical space before core business content.
- Dashboard, CRM and Sales pages surface more useful information above the fold.
- Tables and workspace panels feel more like daily operating surfaces and less like presentation sections.

## Mobile Improvements

- Mobile layouts keep the same hierarchy but reduce excessive card and hero depth.
- Buttons, search controls and cards remain readable and tappable.
- Above-the-fold content is more focused without simply shrinking the desktop experience.

## Components Optimized

- `ProductHero`
- `MetricCard`
- `EntityPageLayout`
- `EntityHeader`
- `EntityTable`
- `EntityToolbar`
- `EntitySearchBar`
- `EntityFilterPanel`
- `ProductActionCard`
- `ProductSectionHeader`
- `FormField`
- `Topbar`
- Dashboard, CRM and Sales workspace surfaces that consume these primitives.

## Design Decisions

- Preserve BOSIACO identity while reducing decorative space.
- Keep hero sections as executive anchors, but make them practical entry points.
- Treat tables as primary professional workspaces by increasing visible density.
- Differentiate executive surfaces from daily workspace cards through spacing instead of new visual systems.
