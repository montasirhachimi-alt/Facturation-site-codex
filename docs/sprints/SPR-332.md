# SPR-332 — Module Identity & Product Personality

## Executive Summary

SPR-332 moves BOSIACO from pure visual consistency toward recognizable module personality. The product now keeps the same premium BOSIACO foundation while giving Dashboard, CRM, Sales, HR and Reports a clearer purpose-driven visual signature.

No Runtime, Services, APIs, Prisma, database, permissions, navigation structure or business logic were changed.

## Module Identities

| Module | Identity | Product Signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Dashboard | Executive command center | Business health, priorities, performance and recent movement. |
| CRM | Relationship map | Companies, contacts, conversations and revenue to defend. |
| Sales | Revenue momentum | Proposals, invoiced revenue, cash and conversion movement. |
| HR | Team pulse | People, absences, leave decisions and organization signals. |
| Reports | Business intelligence | Analytical windows, indicators, exports, charts and rankings. |

## What Changed

- Extended the shared `ProductHero` primitive with module personalities: dashboard, CRM, sales, HR and reports.
- Applied a distinct Sales personality to Quotes, Invoices and Payments without changing routes or workflows.
- Replaced generic HR and Reports headers with purpose-specific product heroes.
- Refined CRM Home hero wording to focus on relationship-building instead of generic commercial control.
- Updated HR metric cards with a people/team visual treatment.
- Updated Reports filters, indicator cards, chart cards and ranking containers with an analytical visual rhythm.

## Visual Decisions

- Module color accents remain subtle and semantic: emerald for Sales momentum, violet for HR people/team context, amber for Reports analysis and cyan/navy for CRM relationships.
- Heroes now carry the strongest module identity, while cards and sections reinforce it without making separate products.
- Section language was made less generic: Sales now speaks about propositions, invoiced revenue and cash; HR speaks about team pulse; Reports speaks about analysis windows and decisions.
- Dashboard remains the executive control surface from SPR-331 and receives the dashboard personality through the shared hero component.
