# HicoPilot Project Vision

## Purpose

Describe the product direction and long-term ambition for HicoPilot.

## Vision

HicoPilot is not a traditional ERP.

HicoPilot is a Business Operating System designed to help small and medium businesses manage daily operations from one intelligent platform.

## Mission

Help SMEs run their business with clarity, speed and confidence from a single modern workspace.

## Product Philosophy

HicoPilot should feel simple, proactive and useful every morning. It should help owners understand what happened, what needs attention and what action should be taken next.

## Core Values

- Simplicity
- Performance
- Security
- AI
- Scalability
- Modern UX

## Long-Term Direction

HicoPilot will evolve from an ERP foundation into a commercial business platform with executive dashboards, core productivity engines, secure business modules, AI assistance and enterprise-grade integrations.
